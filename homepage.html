The story is set in the Capitol and the protagonist of the book is Coriolanus of the great house Snow. The Capitol, a place that once had people living an ostentatious lifestyle is now reduced to dust and rubble. The twelve districts rebelled and as a punishment it was decided the 12 districts, each had to send a male and female tribute to participate in the deadly Hunger Games.The tributes from different districts are pitched against each other in a fight to the death where the last surviving tribute wins the game.
